First thing's first. What's E-store CMS?

Our eStore Complaint Management System is designed to streamline the process of handling and resolving complaints, ensuring your satisfaction. With our user-friendly interface, submitting a complaint is just a few clicks away. Whether you encountered
a problem with a product, faced issues with delivery, or had any other concerns, our system allows you to provide all the necessary details and attach any supporting documents or images.



Features of this complaint management system

02

05

How to make a complaint?

1. Create an account by registering or using google.
2. Go to the Grievance page to upload your complaint.
3. You can add images while filing your complaint.
4. After that you can able to see the complaint details.
5. You can see how much your complaint is processed through track complaint.


What happens after you submit your complaint?

Once you submit your complaint, our dedicated team swings into action. We assign a unique complaint ID to each submission, enabling you to track its progress in real-time. We keep you informed throughout the resolution process, ensuring that you are always up-to-date with the latest developments.
